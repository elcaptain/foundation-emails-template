Your personal packing list
While you ride:

Helmet, cycling shorts, jersey, rain jacket, socks, cycling shoes, buff.

In your backpack (25-30 l):

Spare clothes:
A spare jersey, spare cycling shorts, two pairs of underpants, two pairs of socks.

During the day:
Water bladder, sunscreen, first aid kit, headlamp, bike lock, energy bars and gels, cell phone, power bank, cash, credit card, passport.

During the night:
Sleeping bag liner, sandals/slippers, comfy pants, fleece, T-shirt, toothbrush and paste, charger for your devices.

Extras:
Multi-tool, pump, two spare tubes, patch kit, duct tape & zip ties, brake pads, chain lube, spare derailleur hanger, spare spokes, two shift/brake cables.
